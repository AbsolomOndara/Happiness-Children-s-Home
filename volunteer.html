Volunteer With Us

Your time and skills can bring smiles to 47 children. Join our volunteer family today.

Why Volunteer?

Make a Difference

Directly impact a child's life through your presence and care.

Join a Community

Become part of a loving, supportive team dedicated to children.

Grow Skills

Gain valuable experience in childcare, teaching, and social work.

Spread Love

Share your kindness and see the immediate joy it brings.

Types of Volunteering

On-Site

  • Play and mentor children
  • Assist with homework
  • Help in the kitchen/farm
  • Organize activities

Remote

  • Online tutoring
  • Graphic design
  • Social media support
  • Fundraising campaigns

Skills-Based

  • Medical/dental camps
  • Counseling services
  • Sports coaching
  • Arts & music workshops

Requirements

Minimum 18 years (or accompanied by adult)
Passion for children
At least 2 hours per week
Valid ID / background check
Positive attitude
