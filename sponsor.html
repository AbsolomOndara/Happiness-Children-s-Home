Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose a child to sponsor?
You can browse our "Meet Our Children" section and click "Sponsor" on any child's profile. If you need assistance, contact us and we'll help.
Can I write to my sponsored child?
Absolutely! We encourage sponsors to exchange letters and photos. You can send them via email or post, and we'll deliver them.
What does the sponsorship fee cover?
It covers education, uniform, meals, healthcare, and emotional support. A portion goes towards home maintenance and activities.
Can I visit the home?
Yes, sponsors are welcome to visit by appointment. Please contact us to arrange a visit.
